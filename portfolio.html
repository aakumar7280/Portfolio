About

A warm eye for real moments and meaningful stories.

I am Aarush Kumar, a photographer based in Toronto. Over the last few years, I have been drawn to honest moments: a genuine expression in a portrait, the energy of an event, and the poetry of everyday street scenes.

My approach is collaborative and relaxed, so people feel comfortable in front of the camera. I want each image to feel personal, natural, and timeless - the kind of photograph you return to years later and still feel.
